Section 1: Shipping Policy

Standard Shipping: Orders under $50 incur a flat $5.99 shipping fee. Orders $50 and over qualify for free standard shipping (3-5 business days).

Expedited Shipping: Available for $14.99. Orders placed before 2:00 PM EST will arrive in 1-2 business days.

International Shipping: Currently, AuraTech Electronics only ships within the United States and Canada. International shipping to Canada is a flat rate of $25.00.

Delayed Orders: If an order status is marked as "delayed" for more than 5 business days past the expected delivery date, the customer is entitled to a 15% refund on their order total.

Section 2: Return & Refund Policy

Return Window: Customers can return unused items in their original packaging within 30 days of the delivery date.

Exceptions: Software purchases, gift cards, and opened headphones cannot be returned due to hygiene and licensing restrictions.

Restocking Fee: A 10% restocking fee applies to all returned laptops, desktop computers, monitors, televisions, refrigerators, washing machines, and smartwatches unless the item arrived defective. Mobile phones are subject to a 15% restocking fee if returned after being activated.

Refund Processing: Once a return is received and inspected, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days to the original payment method.

Section 3: Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?

A: Address changes can only be processed if the order status is still "processing." Once the status changes to "in-transit," we cannot alter the destination.

Q: What do I do if my package says delivered, but I haven't received it?

A: Please check with neighbors and your local carrier first. If it has been 48 hours since the delivery notification, contact our support team to open a lost package claim.

Q: Do you offer price matching?

A: Yes, we offer price matching with authorized retailers within 14 days of purchase. The item must be identical and currently in stock on the competitor's website.
